## Free vs. Paid Content Boundaries

### Free / Trial Experience

#### ‚úÖ Fully Free Content

1. **Basic AI News Feed**
   - Simple RSS aggregation
   - Top 10-15 articles per day
   - Basic categorization
   - No personalization
   - No advanced analysis

2. **Basic Email Campaign Analysis**
   - Upload 1 campaign per month
   - High-level insights (open rate, click rate, top performers)
   - Basic recommendations
   - No deep diagnostics
   - No optimization recommendations

3. **Limited Workflows**
   - 3 workflows maximum
   - Basic templates only
   - 100 automations per month
   - No advanced integrations
   - No custom AI agents

4. **Basic Onboarding**
   - Simple welcome flow
   - Basic setup wizard
   - No pre-test
   - No personalization

5. **Public Content**
   - Blog posts (gated: detailed case studies)
   - FAQ (gated: advanced troubleshooting)
   - Basic documentation

## Content Gating Recommendations

### What to Hide or Compress

#### 1. Specific Step-by-Step Playbooks
**Current:** Full workflows explained in detail  
**Action:** Show overview only, gate detailed steps  
**Location:** `/templates`, `/workflows`, blog posts

#### 2. Internal Frameworks
**Current:** "6-Perspective Analysis" fully explained  
**Action:** Mention concept, gate methodology  
**Location:** `/systems-thinking`, `/features`

#### 3. Detailed Templates
**Current:** All templates visible and usable  
**Action:** Show 5 basic, gate rest  
**Location:** `/templates`, onboarding

#### 4. Deep Technical Setups
**Current:** Full integration guides  
**Action:** Show basic setup, gate advanced config  
**Location:** `/integrations`, help docs

#### 5. Full Case Studies
**Current:** Complete case studies visible  
**Action:** Summary + key metrics, gate details  
**Location:** `/case-studies`, blog
