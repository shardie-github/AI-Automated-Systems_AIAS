# Getting Started: Day 1 Guide

Welcome to AIAS Platform! This guide will help you get set up and create your first automation workflow in about 30 minutes.

## What to Expect on Day 1

**Goal:** Connect your first integration and create your first workflow.

**Time Required:** 20-30 minutes

**What You'll Accomplish:**
- ✅ Create your account
- ✅ Connect your first integration (Shopify or Wave Accounting)
- ✅ Create your first automation workflow
- ✅ See your workflow in action

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account (2 minutes)

1. Go to [aias-platform.com/signup](https://aias-platform.com/signup)
2. Sign up with your email or Google account
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile (optional but recommended)

**Tip:** Use a business email address so you can easily connect integrations later.

---

## Step 2: Connect Your First Integration (5-10 minutes)

### Option A: Connect Shopify

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect Shopify**
3. Enter your Shopify store URL (e.g., `yourstore.myshopify.com`)
4. Authorize AIAS to access your store
5. You'll see a confirmation when connected

**What You Can Automate:**
- Order processing
- Inventory updates
- Customer notifications
- Product sync

### Option B: Connect Wave Accounting

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect Wave Accounting**
3. Sign in to your Wave account
4. Authorize AIAS to access your accounting data
5. You'll see a confirmation when connected

**What You Can Automate:**
- Invoice creation
- Payment reminders
- Expense tracking
- Financial reporting

---

## Step 3: Create Your First Workflow (10-15 minutes)

### Using a Template (Recommended)

1. Go to **Workflows** → **Create Workflow**
2. Click **Browse Templates**
3. Choose a template that matches your needs:
   - **Shopify Order Notification** - Get notified when orders come in
   - **Wave Invoice Reminder** - Automatically remind customers about overdue invoices
   - **Daily Business Summary** - Get a daily summary of your business activity

4. Click **Use This Template**
5. Fill in the required fields:
   - Integration settings (which Shopify store, which Wave business)
   - Notification preferences (email, Slack, etc.)
   - Schedule (if applicable)

6. Click **Save & Activate**

### Creating a Custom Workflow

1. Go to **Workflows** → **Create Workflow**
2. Click **Create Custom Workflow**
3. Add a trigger (e.g., "New order in Shopify")
4. Add an action (e.g., "Send email notification")
5. Configure each step
6. Test your workflow
7. Save and activate

---

## Step 4: Test Your Workflow (5 minutes)

1. Go to **Workflows** → Select your workflow
2. Click **Test Workflow** (or wait for a real trigger)
3. Check the execution log to see if it worked
4. Verify the result (check your email, Slack, etc.)

**Troubleshooting:**
- If the workflow fails, check the error message
- Make sure your integration is connected
- Verify your workflow settings are correct
- See our [Troubleshooting Guide](/help/troubleshooting) for common issues
