**Effective Date:** January 15, 2024

## Overview

This policy outlines AIAS Platform's refund and cancellation policy for subscriptions and services.

**Key Points:**
- ✅ **30-Day Money-Back Guarantee:** Full refund within 30 days of initial subscription (first-time subscribers only)
- ✅ **No Contracts:** Cancel anytime, no long-term contracts
- ✅ **No Prorated Refunds:** No refunds for partial billing periods (except 30-day guarantee)
- ✅ **Automatic Renewal:** Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled

---

## Refund Policy

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

**Eligibility:**
- ✅ First-time subscribers only (not applicable to renewals)
- ✅ Within 30 days of initial subscription purchase
- ✅ One refund per customer (lifetime limit)

**Process:**
1. Email support@aias-platform.com with refund request
2. Include your account email and reason for refund (optional)
3. We'll review and process refund within 5-10 business days
4. Refund will be credited to your original payment method

**Refund Amount:**
- **Full Refund:** 100% of subscription fee (including GST/HST)
- **Processing Time:** 5-10 business days (to original payment method)

### Refund Exclusions

**Not Eligible for Refund:**
- ❌ Renewals (subscriptions renewed after initial 30 days)
- ❌ Partial billing periods (no prorated refunds)
- ❌ Cancelled subscriptions (no refunds after cancellation)
- ❌ Free tier (no refunds for free subscriptions)
- ❌ Third-party charges (Stripe fees, bank fees, etc.)

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel

**Option 1: Account Settings**
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings > Billing
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

**Option 2: Email**
1. Email support@aias-platform.com
2. Include your account email and cancellation request
3. We'll process cancellation within 24-48 hours

### Cancellation Effective Date

- **Immediate:** Cancellation takes effect immediately
- **Access:** You retain access until the end of your current billing period
- **No Refund:** No refunds for remaining billing period (except 30-day guarantee)

### What Happens After Cancellation

**Immediate:**
- Subscription cancelled (no further charges)
- Access retained until end of billing period
- Workflows continue to run until end of billing period

**After Billing Period Ends:**
- Access revoked (account downgraded to Free tier)
- Workflows paused (can be reactivated with subscription)
- Data retained for 90 days (can be exported before deletion)
