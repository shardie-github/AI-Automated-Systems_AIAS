Canadian SMBs waste 10+ hours per week on repetitive tasks that AI can automate. Current solutions are either too expensive (CAD $150-500/month), too complex (2+ hours setup), or missing Canadian integrations (Shopify, Wave, RBC).

- Affordable pricing (CAD $49/month vs. $150+ competitors)

- 2M+ Canadian solo operators (underserved market)

**Pricing**

- Starter: CAD $49/month (10 agents, unlimited automations)
