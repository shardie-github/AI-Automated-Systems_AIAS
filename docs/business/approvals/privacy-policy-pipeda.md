## 3. PIPEDA Principles

We adhere to the 10 PIPEDA principles:

### 3.1 Accountability
- We are responsible for personal information under our control
- We have designated a Privacy Officer (contact: privacy@aias-platform.com)
- We train our employees on privacy practices

### 3.2 Identifying Purposes
- We identify the purposes for collecting personal information at or before the time of collection
- We collect personal information only for specified, legitimate purposes

### 3.3 Consent
- We obtain consent before collecting, using, or disclosing personal information
- Consent can be express (explicit) or implied (inferred from conduct)
- You can withdraw consent at any time (subject to legal or contractual restrictions)

### 3.4 Limiting Collection
- We collect only the personal information necessary for identified purposes
- We collect personal information by fair and lawful means

### 3.5 Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention
- We use personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected
- We retain personal information only as long as necessary for identified purposes
- We delete personal information securely when no longer needed

### 3.6 Accuracy
- We keep personal information accurate, complete, and up-to-date
- We update personal information when you notify us of changes

### 3.7 Safeguards
- We protect personal information with appropriate security safeguards
- We use encryption, access controls, and secure storage

### 3.8 Openness
- We make our privacy practices transparent (this Privacy Policy)
- We provide information about our privacy practices upon request

### 3.9 Individual Access
- You have the right to access your personal information
- You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information

### 3.10 Challenging Compliance
- You can challenge our compliance with PIPEDA
- We investigate and respond to privacy complaints
