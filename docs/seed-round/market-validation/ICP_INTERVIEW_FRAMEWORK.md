## ðŸ“‹ INTERVIEW SCRIPT TEMPLATE

### Introduction (2 minutes)
**Script**:
> "Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to chat. I'm [Your Name], [Title] at AIAS Platform. We're building a platform to help businesses automate their workflows without code, and I'd love to get your perspective on some challenges you might be facing.
> 
> This is a research conversation - I'm not trying to sell you anything today. I'm just trying to understand your world better. Does that work for you?
> 
> The call will take about 30 minutes, and I'll send you a summary of what we discuss. Sound good?"

### Section 1: Current State (10 minutes)

**Question 1.1**: "Tell me about your role and what you're responsible for."
- **Follow-ups**: 
  - What does a typical day/week look like?
  - What are your biggest priorities right now?

**Question 1.2**: "What manual or repetitive tasks do you or your team handle regularly?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - How much time do you spend on these tasks?
  - What happens if these tasks don't get done?
  - How do you feel about doing these tasks?

**Question 1.3**: "What tools or systems do you use to manage your work?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - How well do these tools work together?
  - What's missing or frustrating about your current setup?
  - Have you tried to automate anything? What happened?

### Section 2: Problem Validation (10 minutes)

**Question 2.1**: "If you could wave a magic wand and automate one process, what would it be?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - Why is this process important?
  - What would you do with the time you'd save?
  - How much would that be worth to you?

**Question 2.2**: "Have you tried to solve this problem before? What happened?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - What solutions did you try?
  - Why didn't they work?
  - What would need to be different for a solution to work?

**Question 2.3**: "On a scale of 1-10, how painful is this problem for you?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - What would need to change to make it a 10?
  - What would make it less painful (a 5 or lower)?

### Section 3: Solution Validation (8 minutes)

**Question 3.1**: "I'm going to describe something we're building. Tell me what you think."
- **Pitch**: "We're building a platform where you can create automated workflows between your tools - like when a new order comes in Shopify, automatically send a notification to Slack, create a record in your CRM, and send a confirmation email - all without writing code. You can build these workflows visually in minutes."
- **Follow-ups**:
  - What's your initial reaction?
  - Does this solve the problem we discussed?
  - What questions do you have?

**Question 3.2**: "If this existed today, would you use it?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - What would need to be true for you to use it?
  - What would prevent you from using it?
  - Who else would need to be involved in the decision?

**Question 3.3**: "What would you expect to pay for something like this?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - Is that per month, per year, one-time?
  - What's your budget for tools like this?
  - Who controls that budget?

### Section 4: Closing & Next Steps (5 minutes)

**Question 4.1**: "Is there anything else I should know about your situation?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - Any other pain points I should be aware of?
  - Any other tools or solutions you're considering?

**Question 4.2**: "Would you be open to being an early beta tester when we launch?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - What would you need to see to try it?
  - Would you be willing to pay for it, or would you need it to be free initially?

**Question 4.3**: "Is there anyone else I should talk to who has similar challenges?"
- **Follow-ups**:
  - Can I mention your name when I reach out?
  - Any other companies or people you'd recommend?

**Closing**:
> "Thank you so much for your time and insights. This has been really helpful. I'll send you a summary of what we discussed, and I'll keep you updated on our progress. Would you like to stay in touch?"
