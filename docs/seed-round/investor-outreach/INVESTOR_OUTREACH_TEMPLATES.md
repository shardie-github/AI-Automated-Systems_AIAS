## BEST PRACTICES

### Email Best Practices:
1. **Personalize**: Always personalize - mention specific portfolio companies, investments, or connections
2. **Keep It Short**: 3-4 paragraphs max, get to the point quickly
3. **Clear Ask**: Make it clear what you're asking for (meeting, intro, etc.)
4. **Follow Up**: Follow up 1 week, 2 weeks, then monthly if no response
5. **Track**: Track all outreach in CRM or spreadsheet

### Timing:
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times**: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM (recipient's timezone)
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, holidays

### Subject Lines:
- Keep it short and clear
- Include company name
- Mention "Seed Round" or "Opportunity"
- Avoid spammy words (FREE, URGENT, etc.)
