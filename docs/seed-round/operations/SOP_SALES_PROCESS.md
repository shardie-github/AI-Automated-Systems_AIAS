# Standard Operating Procedure: Sales Process
## SOP-001: End-to-End Sales Process

**Version**: 1.0  
**Last Updated**: [Date]  
**Owner**: VP Sales  
**Review Frequency**: Quarterly

---

## PURPOSE

This SOP defines the standardized sales process from prospecting to close, ensuring consistent execution, predictable outcomes, and scalable growth.

---

## SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- Sales Development Representatives (SDRs)
- Account Executives (AEs)
- Sales Managers
- Customer Success (for handoff)

---

## PROCESS OVERVIEW

```
Prospecting → Qualification → Discovery → Demo → Proposal → Negotiation → Close → Handoff
```

**Target Sales Cycle**: 30-60 days  
**Target Win Rate**: 25-30%

---

## DETAILED PROCESS

### Stage 1: Prospecting

**Owner**: SDR  
**Duration**: Ongoing  
**Goal**: Generate qualified leads

**Activities**:
1. **Research** (15 min per prospect):
   - Identify target companies (ICP fit)
   - Research company and decision makers
   - Research current tools and pain points
   - Find contact information

2. **Outreach** (5 min per prospect):
   - Craft personalized email/cold call
   - Send initial touchpoint
   - Log in CRM

3. **Follow-Up** (3-5 touches):
   - Touch 1: Initial email (Day 0)
   - Touch 2: LinkedIn message (Day 3)
   - Touch 3: Follow-up email (Day 7)
   - Touch 4: Phone call (Day 10)
   - Touch 5: Final email (Day 14)

**Success Criteria**: Meeting scheduled

**Tools**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Outreach.io, HubSpot

**Metrics**:
- Outbound touches: 50+ per week
- Response rate: 5-10%
- Meeting rate: 2-5%

---

### Stage 2: Qualification

**Owner**: SDR/AE  
**Duration**: 15-30 minutes  
**Goal**: Determine if prospect is qualified

**Activities**:
1. **BANT Qualification**:
   - **Budget**: Do they have budget? What's the range?
   - **Authority**: Are we talking to the decision maker?
   - **Need**: Do they have a clear pain point?
   - **Timeline**: When do they need to solve this?

2. **ICP Fit Assessment**:
   - Company size: 10-500 employees
   - Industry: Target industry
   - Current tools: Using relevant tools
   - Pain points: Match our solution

3. **Document in CRM**:
   - Update deal stage
   - Add qualification notes
   - Set next steps

**Success Criteria**: All BANT criteria met + ICP fit

**Disqualification Criteria**:
- No budget
- No decision maker
- No clear need
- No timeline
- Poor ICP fit

**Metrics**:
- Qualification rate: 20-30% of prospects
- Time to qualify: <30 minutes

---

### Stage 3: Discovery

**Owner**: AE  
**Duration**: 30-45 minutes  
**Goal**: Understand customer's specific needs

**Activities**:
1. **Pre-Call Preparation** (15 min):
   - Review CRM notes
   - Research company and contact
   - Prepare discovery questions
   - Identify potential use cases

2. **Discovery Call** (30-45 min):
   - Opening: Build rapport, set agenda
   - Current State: Understand their processes
   - Pain Points: Identify specific challenges
   - Impact: Quantify time/cost of problems
   - Solution Validation: Gauge interest
   - Decision Process: Understand buying process
   - Next Steps: Schedule demo

3. **Post-Call** (15 min):
   - Document notes in CRM
   - Identify use cases
   - Prepare demo plan
   - Send follow-up email

**Success Criteria**: Clear understanding of needs, use cases identified, demo scheduled

**Tools**: Discovery question framework, CRM, screen recording

**Metrics**:
- Discovery → Demo conversion: 50%
- Average discovery call duration: 35 minutes

---

### Stage 4: Demo

**Owner**: AE  
**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Demonstrate value through specific use cases

**Activities**:
1. **Pre-Demo Preparation** (30 min):
   - Review discovery notes
   - Identify 2-3 specific use cases
   - Prepare relevant workflows
   - Test demo environment
   - Prepare screen sharing

2. **Demo Execution** (30 min):
   - Introduction (2 min)
   - Problem Recap (3 min)
   - Platform Overview (5 min)
   - Use Case Demo (15 min)
   - Q&A (3 min)
   - Next Steps (2 min)

3. **Post-Demo** (15 min):
   - Document feedback in CRM
   - Address objections
   - Send follow-up email with resources
   - Schedule next steps

**Success Criteria**: Positive response, next steps agreed, proposal requested

**Tools**: Demo environment, screen sharing, battlecards

**Metrics**:
- Demo → Proposal conversion: 40%
- Average demo duration: 30 minutes

---

### Stage 5: Proposal

**Owner**: AE  
**Duration**: 2-3 days  
**Goal**: Present formal proposal with pricing

**Activities**:
1. **Proposal Creation** (1-2 hours):
   - Customize proposal template
   - Include ROI calculation
   - Address specific use cases
   - Include pricing and terms
   - Add relevant case studies

2. **Proposal Delivery**:
   - Send proposal via email
   - Schedule follow-up call
   - Set decision timeline

3. **Follow-Up**:
   - Follow up within 48 hours
   - Address questions/concerns
   - Negotiate if needed

**Success Criteria**: Proposal sent, decision timeline set

**Tools**: Proposal template, ROI calculator, CRM

**Metrics**:
- Proposal → Close conversion: 30%
- Average time to decision: 7-14 days

---

### Stage 6: Negotiation

**Owner**: AE (with Sales Manager approval)  
**Duration**: 3-7 days  
**Goal**: Finalize terms and address concerns

**Activities**:
1. **Address Concerns**:
   - Pricing objections
   - Feature requests
   - Contract terms
   - Timeline concerns

2. **Negotiation**:
   - Understand their constraints
   - Propose alternatives
   - Get manager approval (if needed)
   - Finalize terms

3. **Documentation**:
   - Update CRM with terms
   - Prepare contract
   - Set closing date

**Success Criteria**: Terms agreed, contract ready

**Tools**: Pricing flexibility matrix, contract template, CRM

**Metrics**:
- Negotiation → Close conversion: 80%
- Average discount: 0-10%

---

### Stage 7: Close

**Owner**: AE  
**Duration**: 1-3 days  
**Goal**: Execute contract and onboard customer

**Activities**:
1. **Contract Execution**:
   - Send contract for signature
   - Follow up on signature
   - Process payment
   - Confirm start date

2. **Handoff to Customer Success**:
   - Schedule onboarding call
   - Share customer context
   - Transfer CRM ownership
   - Introduce CSM

3. **Post-Close**:
   - Celebrate win
   - Update pipeline
   - Request referral
   - Add to case study pipeline (if applicable)

**Success Criteria**: Contract signed, payment received, onboarding scheduled

**Tools**: DocuSign, payment processing, CRM

**Metrics**:
- Close rate: 25-30%
- Average time to close: 30-60 days

---

## ESCALATION PROCEDURES

### Pricing Discounts:
- **0-10%**: AE can approve
- **10-20%**: Sales Manager approval required
- **20%+**: VP Sales approval required

### Contract Changes:
- **Standard terms**: AE can approve
- **Non-standard terms**: Legal review required
- **Enterprise terms**: VP Sales + Legal approval

### Lost Deals:
- **Document reason** in CRM
- **Review with manager** if >$10K ARR
- **Post-mortem** for strategic losses

---

## QUALITY ASSURANCE

### Deal Reviews:
- **Weekly**: Pipeline review with manager
- **Monthly**: Forecast review
- **Quarterly**: Process review and optimization

### CRM Hygiene:
- **Daily**: Update deal stage, add notes
- **Weekly**: Review and clean pipeline
- **Monthly**: Archive closed/lost deals
