# Standard Operating Procedure: Customer Success
## SOP-002: Onboarding, Support, and Retention

**Version**: 1.0  
**Last Updated**: [Date]  
**Owner**: VP Customer Success  
**Review Frequency**: Quarterly

---

## PURPOSE

This SOP defines the standardized customer success process from onboarding to renewal, ensuring high customer satisfaction, low churn, and successful expansion.

---

## SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- Customer Success Managers (CSMs)
- Support Team
- Onboarding Specialists
- Account Managers

---

## PROCESS OVERVIEW

```
Onboarding → Adoption → Success → Expansion → Renewal
```

**Target Metrics**:
- **Time-to-Value**: <5 minutes (first workflow)
- **Adoption Rate**: 80%+ active users within 30 days
- **Churn Rate**: <5% monthly
- **Net Revenue Retention**: >110%

---

## DETAILED PROCESS

### Stage 1: Onboarding (Days 0-30)

**Owner**: CSM  
**Duration**: 30 days  
**Goal**: Get customer to first successful workflow

**Activities**:

**Day 0: Welcome**
- [ ] Send welcome email with resources
- [ ] Schedule onboarding call
- [ ] Provide access credentials
- [ ] Share getting started guide

**Day 1: Onboarding Call (30 min)**
- [ ] Introduction and rapport building
- [ ] Review customer's goals and use cases
- [ ] Walk through platform basics
- [ ] Identify first workflow to build
- [ ] Set success metrics
- [ ] Schedule check-in calls

**Day 3: First Workflow Support**
- [ ] Check in on progress
- [ ] Help build first workflow
- [ ] Ensure first workflow is running
- [ ] Celebrate "aha moment"

**Day 7: Check-In**
- [ ] Review progress
- [ ] Address questions
- [ ] Identify additional workflows
- [ ] Gather feedback

**Day 14: Mid-Onboarding Review**
- [ ] Review adoption metrics
- [ ] Assess health score
- [ ] Identify at-risk customers
- [ ] Plan additional workflows

**Day 30: Onboarding Completion**
- [ ] Review goals and achievements
- [ ] Confirm customer is successful
- [ ] Transition to ongoing success
- [ ] Schedule quarterly business review

**Success Criteria**:
- First workflow built and running
- Customer understands platform basics
- Health score: Green
- Customer satisfaction: 8+/10

**Tools**: Onboarding checklist, health score dashboard, CRM

**Metrics**:
- Time-to-first-workflow: <5 minutes
- Onboarding completion rate: 90%+
- Customer satisfaction (Day 30): 8+/10

---

### Stage 2: Adoption (Days 31-90)

**Owner**: CSM  
**Duration**: 60 days  
**Goal**: Drive platform adoption and usage

**Activities**:

**Weekly Check-Ins** (Weeks 5-12):
- [ ] Review usage metrics
- [ ] Identify adoption blockers
- [ ] Suggest new workflows
- [ ] Share best practices
- [ ] Address support tickets

**Monthly Business Review** (Month 2-3):
- [ ] Review ROI and value delivered
- [ ] Discuss expansion opportunities
- [ ] Gather feedback
- [ ] Plan next quarter

**Adoption Campaigns**:
- [ ] Feature adoption emails
- [ ] Workflow templates
- [ ] Best practices webinars
- [ ] Customer community access

**Success Criteria**:
- 80%+ active users
- 3+ workflows running
- Health score: Green
- No churn risk

**Tools**: Adoption dashboard, health score, email campaigns

**Metrics**:
- Active user rate: 80%+
- Average workflows per customer: 3+
- Feature adoption rate: 60%+

---

### Stage 3: Success (Days 91-365)

**Owner**: CSM  
**Duration**: Ongoing  
**Goal**: Ensure ongoing value and satisfaction

**Activities**:

**Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)**:
- [ ] Review ROI and metrics
- [ ] Discuss goals and priorities
- [ ] Identify expansion opportunities
- [ ] Gather feedback
- [ ] Plan next quarter

**Monthly Health Checks**:
- [ ] Review health score
- [ ] Check usage trends
- [ ] Identify at-risk customers
- [ ] Proactive outreach

**Ongoing Support**:
- [ ] Respond to support tickets (<4 hour SLA)
- [ ] Proactive workflow optimization
- [ ] Feature education
- [ ] Best practices sharing

**Success Criteria**:
- Health score: Green
- Customer satisfaction: 8+/10
- No churn risk
- Expansion opportunities identified

**Tools**: Health score dashboard, support ticketing, CRM

**Metrics**:
- Health score: 80%+ Green
- Customer satisfaction: 8+/10
- Support response time: <4 hours
- Churn risk: <10%

---

### Stage 4: Expansion (Ongoing)

**Owner**: CSM / Account Manager  
**Duration**: Ongoing  
**Goal**: Identify and close expansion opportunities

**Activities**:

**Expansion Identification**:
- [ ] Review usage trends (approaching limits)
- [ ] Identify new use cases
- [ ] Assess feature needs
- [ ] Evaluate tier upgrade potential

**Expansion Outreach**:
- [ ] Schedule expansion discussion
- [ ] Present ROI case for upgrade
- [ ] Address concerns
- [ ] Close expansion deal

**Success Criteria**:
- Expansion opportunities identified: 30%+ of customers
- Expansion close rate: 40%+
- Average expansion: 20-30% ARR increase

**Tools**: Usage analytics, expansion playbook, CRM

**Metrics**:
- Expansion rate: 30%+ of customers
- Expansion close rate: 40%+
- Average expansion value: 20-30% ARR

---

### Stage 5: Renewal (90 days before renewal)

**Owner**: CSM / Account Manager  
**Duration**: 90 days  
**Goal**: Secure renewal and prevent churn

**Activities**:

**90 Days Before Renewal**:
- [ ] Review account health
- [ ] Identify renewal risk
- [ ] Schedule renewal discussion
- [ ] Prepare renewal proposal

**60 Days Before Renewal**:
- [ ] Present renewal proposal
- [ ] Address concerns
- [ ] Negotiate terms (if needed)
- [ ] Set renewal timeline

**30 Days Before Renewal**:
- [ ] Follow up on renewal
- [ ] Finalize terms
- [ ] Execute renewal contract
- [ ] Celebrate renewal

**Success Criteria**:
- Renewal rate: 90%+
- No surprise churn
- Expansion included (if applicable)

**Tools**: Renewal playbook, contract template, CRM

**Metrics**:
- Renewal rate: 90%+
- Time to renew: 30-60 days
- Expansion at renewal: 20%+ of renewals

## CUSTOMER HEALTH SCORING

### Health Score Components:

**Usage (40%)**:
- Active users: 80%+ = Green, 50-79% = Yellow, <50% = Red
- Workflows running: 3+ = Green, 1-2 = Yellow, 0 = Red
- Feature adoption: 60%+ = Green, 40-59% = Yellow, <40% = Red

**Engagement (30%)**:
- Support tickets: <2/month = Green, 2-5 = Yellow, >5 = Red
- QBR attendance: Yes = Green, No = Red
- Response to outreach: <24 hours = Green, 24-48 = Yellow, >48 = Red

**Value (20%)**:
- ROI achieved: Yes = Green, Partial = Yellow, No = Red
- Goals met: Yes = Green, Partial = Yellow, No = Red

**Satisfaction (10%)**:
- NPS: 8+ = Green, 6-7 = Yellow, <6 = Red
- CSAT: 8+ = Green, 6-7 = Yellow, <6 = Red

**Overall Health Score**:
- **Green (80-100)**: Healthy, low churn risk
- **Yellow (60-79)**: At-risk, needs attention
- **Red (<60)**: High churn risk, immediate action required

## SUPPORT PROCESS

### Support Ticket Triage:

**P1 - Critical** (Response: <1 hour, Resolution: <4 hours):
- Platform down
- Security issue
- Data loss
- Payment failure

**P2 - High** (Response: <4 hours, Resolution: <24 hours):
- Workflow not working
- Integration issue
- Feature not working as expected

**P3 - Medium** (Response: <24 hours, Resolution: <3 days):
- How-to questions
- Feature requests
- General inquiries

**P4 - Low** (Response: <3 days, Resolution: <7 days):
- Documentation requests
- Non-urgent feedback

### Support Escalation:
1. **Level 1**: Support team (tickets)
2. **Level 2**: CSM (customer relationship)
3. **Level 3**: Engineering (technical issues)
4. **Level 4**: VP Customer Success (critical issues)

---

## CHURN PREVENTION

### At-Risk Customer Identification:
- Health score: Yellow or Red
- Usage decline: >50% drop
- No activity: >30 days
- Support tickets: Unresolved or increasing
- Negative feedback

### Intervention Process:
1. **Identify**: Health score or usage alerts
2. **Assess**: Review account, identify root cause
3. **Engage**: Schedule call with CSM
4. **Resolve**: Address issues, provide support
5. **Follow-up**: Monitor recovery, adjust health score

### Churn Post-Mortem:
- **Document**: Reason for churn
- **Analyze**: Root cause analysis
- **Learn**: Identify process improvements
- **Share**: Team learnings
