## INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] ("Effective Date") by and between:

**AIAS Platform, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and

**[ASSIGNOR NAME]** ("Assignor"), an individual residing at [ADDRESS].

---

### RECITALS

WHEREAS, Assignor is [FOUNDER/EMPLOYEE/CONTRACTOR] of the Company;

WHEREAS, Assignor may create, develop, or contribute to intellectual property in connection with Assignor's relationship with the Company;

WHEREAS, the Company desires to acquire all right, title, and interest in and to such intellectual property;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

---

### 1. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**1.1 Assignment**. Assignor hereby assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company, and the Company hereby accepts, all right, title, and interest in and to all Intellectual Property (as defined below) that:

(a) Assignor has created, developed, or contributed to, in whole or in part, during the term of Assignor's relationship with the Company;

(b) Relates to the Company's business, products, services, or technology;

(c) Was created using Company resources, time, or information; or

(d) Was created during Company time or using Company equipment, facilities, or materials.

**1.2 Definition of Intellectual Property**. For purposes of this Agreement, "Intellectual Property" includes, but is not limited to:

- Inventions, discoveries, and improvements (whether patentable or not);
- Works of authorship, including software code, documentation, designs, and content;
- Trade secrets and confidential information;
- Trademarks, service marks, and trade names;
- Copyrights and copyrightable works;
- Know-how, processes, and methodologies;
- Data, databases, and compilations;
- Any other intellectual property rights recognized under applicable law.

---

### 2. EXCLUDED INVENTIONS

**2.1 Prior Inventions**. Assignor has listed on **Exhibit A** attached hereto all inventions, works, and intellectual property that:

(a) Were created by Assignor prior to the Effective Date;

(b) Are owned by Assignor or a third party; and

(c) Are not assigned to the Company under this Agreement.

**2.2 Excluded Inventions**. The following are excluded from this Agreement:

(a) Inventions that do not relate to the Company's business or research;

(b) Inventions that do not result from work performed for the Company;

(c) Inventions developed entirely on Assignor's own time without using Company equipment, supplies, facilities, or trade secret information; and

(d) Inventions that do not relate to the Company's actual or demonstrably anticipated research, development, or business.

---

### 3. FURTHER ASSURANCES

**3.1 Cooperation**. Assignor agrees to:

(a) Execute and deliver any additional documents, instruments, or assignments that may be necessary to perfect the Company's ownership of the Intellectual Property;

(b) Assist the Company in obtaining, maintaining, and enforcing patents, copyrights, trademarks, and other intellectual property rights;

(c) Testify in any proceeding related to the Intellectual Property; and

(d) Perform all other acts reasonably requested by the Company to effectuate the purposes of this Agreement.

**3.2 Power of Attorney**. If the Company is unable to secure Assignor's signature on any document necessary to perfect the Company's ownership, Assignor hereby irrevocably designates and appoints the Company and its duly authorized officers and agents as Assignor's agent and attorney-in-fact to act for and on Assignor's behalf to execute and file such documents.

---

### 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Assignor represents and warrants that:

(a) Assignor has the full right, power, and authority to enter into this Agreement and to assign the Intellectual Property to the Company;

(b) The Intellectual Property is original to Assignor or Assignor has obtained all necessary rights and permissions;

(c) The Intellectual Property does not infringe upon the rights of any third party;

(d) Assignor has not granted and will not grant any rights in the Intellectual Property to any third party; and

(e) There are no claims, liens, or encumbrances on the Intellectual Property.

---

### 5. CONSIDERATION

In consideration for the assignment of Intellectual Property, Assignor acknowledges receipt of:

- [ ] Employment/Consulting Agreement with the Company
- [ ] Stock options or equity grants (see Stock Option Agreement)
- [ ] Salary/compensation
- [ ] Other consideration: [DESCRIBE]

Assignor acknowledges that the consideration received is fair and adequate.

---

### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Assignor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all Company intellectual property and trade secrets, both during and after Assignor's relationship with the Company, in accordance with the Company's Confidentiality Agreement.

---

### 7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

---

### 8. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement, together with any exhibits attached hereto, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations.

---

### 9. AMENDMENT

This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by both parties.

---

### 10. SEVERABILITY

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

---

### 11. COUNTERPARTS

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, but all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
