Real-world scenarios where AIAS solves actual business problems.

## 1. E-Commerce Inventory Management

**The Problem:**
You sell products on multiple platforms—your website, Amazon, eBay, Shopify. Inventory counts get out of sync. You oversell products. You lose sales because items show as out of stock when they're actually available.

**How AIAS Solves It:**
- Automatically sync inventory across all platforms in real-time
- Update product availability instantly when items are sold
- Send alerts when inventory drops below threshold
- Generate daily inventory reports automatically

**The Outcome:**
- Zero overselling incidents
- 30% increase in sales from better inventory visibility
- 10 hours per week saved on manual inventory management
- Complete confidence in your stock levels

## 5. Financial Reporting and Analysis

**The Problem:**
You need financial reports regularly, but pulling data from multiple sources is time-consuming. By the time reports are ready, the data is outdated. You're making decisions based on incomplete information.

**How AIAS Solves It:**
- Automatically pull data from Stripe, PayPal, bank accounts, and accounting software
- Generate daily, weekly, and monthly reports automatically
- Calculate key metrics (MRR, churn, LTV, CAC) automatically
- Send reports to stakeholders automatically
- Alert on anomalies or trends that need attention

**The Outcome:**
- Real-time visibility into financial health
- Hours saved every week on report generation
- Faster decision-making with up-to-date data
- Better financial planning and forecasting
