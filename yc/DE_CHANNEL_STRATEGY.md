## Channel Prioritization

### Primary Channel: Shopify App Store
- **Priority:** High
- **Reason:** Largest distribution channel, high intent users
- **Goal:** 100 installs/month → 20 activations → 4 paying customers

### Secondary Channel: SEO/Content Marketing
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Reason:** Long-term organic growth, low CAC
- **Goal:** 1,000 organic visitors/month → 50 signups → 10 paying customers

### Tertiary Channel: Referrals
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Reason:** Viral growth mechanism, $0 CAC
- **Goal:** 10% of users refer 1 friend/year → 100 referrals/year → 50 paying customers
