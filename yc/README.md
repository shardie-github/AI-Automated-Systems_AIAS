## Key Metrics to Know Cold

**Usage:**
- DAU, WAU, MAU
- Activation Rate
- Retention (Day 7, Day 30)

**Growth:**
- Signups (weekly, monthly)
- MRR Growth (MoM %)
- Paying Customers

**Unit Economics:**
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV:CAC Ratio (target: 3:1)
- Gross Margin (target: 80%+)

**See `YC_METRICS_CHECKLIST.md` for full list and definitions.**
