## Validation Questions

### Problem Validation
1. "How many hours per week do you spend on order processing?"
2. "What tasks take the most time? (order confirmations, shipping labels, inventory)"
3. "What happens if you don't do these tasks?"
4. "Have you tried to automate these tasks? What happened?"

### Persona Validation
5. "What's your annual revenue?"
6. "How many employees do you have?"
7. "What tools do you currently use? (Shopify, accounting, email)"
8. "What's your budget for business tools?"

### Solution Validation
9. "If I could automate order processing for you, would that be valuable?"
10. "How much time would that save you per week?"
11. "What would you do with that extra time?"

### Willingness to Pay
12. "How much would you pay per month to automate order processing?"
13. "What's your current budget for business tools?"
14. "What would make you switch from your current solution?"
