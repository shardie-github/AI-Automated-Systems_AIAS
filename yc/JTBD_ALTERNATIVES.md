## Job 1: Automate Order Processing

### Competing Alternatives

**Alternative 1: Zapier**
- **Why Users Choose:** Known brand, many integrations
- **Limitations:** $50/month for just 5 automations, technical setup
- **Switching Trigger:** Too expensive, need more automations, want Canadian integrations

**Alternative 2: Make (formerly Integromat)**
- **Why Users Choose:** Lower price ($9-29/month), many integrations
- **Limitations:** Complex setup, requires technical expertise
- **Switching Trigger:** Too complex, want easier solution, want Canadian integrations

**Alternative 3: Manual Process**
- **Why Users Choose:** Free, familiar, no learning curve
- **Limitations:** Time-consuming (3 hours/day), error-prone
- **Switching Trigger:** Too time-consuming, want to scale, want to reduce errors

**Alternative 4: Custom Development**
- **Why Users Choose:** Custom solution, fits exact needs
- **Limitations:** Expensive ($10K-50K), takes months, requires maintenance
- **Switching Trigger:** Too expensive, want faster solution, want affordable option

**Why AIAS Wins:**
- Affordable ($49/month unlimited vs. $50/month for 5 automations)
- No-code (visual builder vs. technical setup)
- Canadian-first (native integrations vs. limited Canadian integrations)

## Job 2: Generate Proposals Automatically

### Competing Alternatives

**Alternative 1: Manual Writing**
- **Why Users Choose:** Free, full control, no learning curve
- **Limitations:** Time-consuming (2 hours per proposal), quality varies
- **Switching Trigger:** Too time-consuming, want consistency, want to scale

**Alternative 2: ChatGPT/Claude**
- **Why Users Choose:** Free/low cost, AI-powered, fast
- **Limitations:** Not integrated with workflow, requires copy-paste, no templates
- **Switching Trigger:** Want integration, want templates, want automation

**Alternative 3: Proposal Software (Proposify, PandaDoc)**
- **Why Users Choose:** Purpose-built, professional templates
- **Limitations:** Expensive ($19-49/month), separate tool, no automation
- **Switching Trigger:** Too expensive, want automation, want all-in-one

**Why AIAS Wins:**
- Integrated (part of workflow vs. separate tool)
- Automated (triggers automatically vs. manual)
- Affordable (included in $49/month vs. $19-49/month separate)

## Job 3: Qualify Leads Automatically

### Competing Alternatives

**Alternative 1: Manual Qualification**
- **Why Users Choose:** Free, full control, personal touch
- **Limitations:** Time-consuming (1 hour per lead), doesn't scale
- **Switching Trigger:** Too time-consuming, want to scale, want consistency

**Alternative 2: CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce)**
- **Why Users Choose:** Purpose-built, professional features
- **Limitations:** Expensive ($20-150/month), complex setup, no AI qualification
- **Switching Trigger:** Too expensive, want AI qualification, want automation

**Alternative 3: Lead Qualification Tools**
- **Why Users Choose:** Purpose-built, AI-powered
- **Limitations:** Expensive ($50-200/month), separate tool, no integration
- **Switching Trigger:** Too expensive, want integration, want all-in-one

**Why AIAS Wins:**
- Integrated (part of workflow vs. separate tool)
- Automated (AI qualification vs. manual)
- Affordable (included in $49/month vs. $50-200/month separate)
