## Customer Interviews

### Interview Plan
- **Target:** 20 customer interviews
- **Segments:** Shopify store owners (10), Consultants (5), Real estate agents (5)
- **Format:** 30-minute video calls
- **Questions:** See interview script below

### Interview Script

**Opening:**
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time. I'm building a product to help Canadian SMBs automate repetitive tasks, and I'd love to get your feedback."

**Problem Validation:**
1. "What repetitive tasks do you spend the most time on?"
2. "How many hours per week do you spend on [specific task]?"
3. "What happens if you don't do these tasks?"
4. "Have you tried to automate these tasks? What happened?"

**Solution Validation:**
5. "If I could automate [specific task] for you, would that be valuable?"
6. "How much time would that save you per week?"
7. "What would you do with that extra time?"

**Willingness to Pay:**
8. "How much would you pay per month to automate [specific task]?"
9. "What's your current budget for business tools?"
10. "What would make you switch from your current solution?"

**Closing:**
11. "Would you be interested in trying our product when it's ready?"
12. "Can I follow up with you in a few weeks?"
