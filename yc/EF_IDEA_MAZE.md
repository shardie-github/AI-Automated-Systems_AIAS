## Reasoning Behind Current Approach

### Why Canadian-First?
- **Market Insight:** Canadian SMBs need Canadian integrations (Shopify, Wave, RBC, TD)
- **Competitive Advantage:** US-focused tools don't have Canadian integrations
- **Compliance:** PIPEDA compliance required for Canadian market
- **Result:** Defensible niche, strong differentiation

### Why $49/Month Pricing?
- **Market Research:** SMBs can't afford $150-500/month enterprise tools
- **Competitive Analysis:** Zapier costs $50/month for just 5 automations
- **Value Proposition:** $49/month for unlimited automations
- **Result:** Affordable pricing, clear value proposition

### Why Visual Workflow Builder?
- **User Research:** Non-technical users need no-code solution
- **Competitive Analysis:** Zapier/Make require technical setup
- **User Experience:** Drag-and-drop is more intuitive
- **Result:** Lower barrier to entry, higher adoption
